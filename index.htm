About Me

Hi, my name is Petula. I’m a creative and resourceful team player with a proven background using research and design thinking to produce high impact interface design and engaging user experiences.

I have past web, DVD and printable design experience with a lateral step into the medical field as a full-time Emergency Medical Tech (EMT) to fulfill a personal dream to help others. I loved every minute of the Emergency Medical Service (EMS) field yet decided to follow my heart and come back to my first love - Design.

Why pursue a career in UX? A few years ago, I read an old article about Object, View, and Interaction Design (OVID) and GUI written by Dick Berry, an IBM engineer. At the time, it was a little above my head but I understood much of it and kept reading more of his articles on the subject. These readings pointed me in the direction of UI and UX. I was hooked! I love the idea, research, problem-solving and work of building a bridge between the user and product.

Currently, I'm back in college to attend Santa Monica College’s B.S. Interaction Design program. In the future, I plan to attend a masters program in HCI and specialize in the research aspect of design.

In my spare time, I'm with my friends/family, having mini adventures, drawing, laughing and exploring the world.
